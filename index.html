LOKOLE: EMAIL FOR ALL

Lokole is Ascoderu's custom device that brings affordable email to rural communities in Africa.

As for the UN Sustainable Development Goals 9: industry, innovation and infrastructures, we also believe that emails can speed up the process of local development by improving education, health services and economic opportunities.

THINK ABOUT THIS PAGE!

Lokole is a device built on RasberryPi/OrangePi by Ascoderu. Lokole is a small portable plug-and-play device ready to work wherever there's cellular coverage. Lokole is ready to change the life of many via our testing and deployment!
